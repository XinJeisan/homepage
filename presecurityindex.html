👤 About

👤 自己紹介

I live with my wife in Sagamihara, Kanagawa, Japan. Somehow I've been here for more than 10 years. Before that I lived in San Mateo, where I was born, followed by Los Angeles, Singapore, Ojiya, Yokohama, and San Diego. I like traveling, either far way by train, bus, or ferry, or nearby by bicycle. While traveling I check out sites from the Tora-san films. In addition to making many friends because of my interest in Tora-san, I have been on TV here in Japan not once but twice because of Tora-san. I also enjoy checking out churches, temples, shrines as well as locally owned shops and restaurants as well as museums. I was able to do all of that in 2024 on a trip through the Goto Islands during Holy Week. Recently on Sundays you can find me at one of our nearby Catholic Churches for Sunday Mass followed by my Japanese lesson in Machida, as I continue to work towards passing the JLPT level N1.

カリフォルニア州で育ち、ロサンゼルス、シンガポール、小千谷、横浜、ラホヤに住んできました。現在は神奈川県相模原市に拠点を置き、英語教育や翻訳を通じて、言語や文化をつなぐ仕事をしています。趣味は長距離サイクリングと読書です。

🌍 Community Involvement

🌍 地域での活動

I am a member of my local neighorhood association, known as chonaikai in Japanese as well as particpate in a daily, neibourhood rajio taiso. I have also paricipated in encouraging an inclusive society while I lived in Japan. Going back to when I was a participaton the JET programme in the late 1990s in Niigata, I helped organize meetings with the local non-Japanese population, which lead to local Japanese classes as well as a multilingual poster on the rules of garbage collection. More recently, I have paricipated on advisory panels for the Governor of Kanagawa and the City of Sagamihara to discuss issues and propose policies to promote and encourage an inclusive society at the prefectural and municipal level.

2016年から神奈川県国際施策推進懇談会の委員を務め、現在は相模原市で多文化共生の推進に取り組んでいます。地域社会の多様性を支えるための提案を続けています。

💼 What I do

💼 仕事

  • Teaching pays the bills but it is also what I love doing. I have been teaching since I came over to Japan on the JET programme and have taught all levels, from elementary school to university students as well as adults. Teaching in a traditional school setting can be stressful, working with both children and adults who haven't chosen to be together, all with different ideas about what they should be learning and how they should be learning. It can be frustrating for people who want to rise above medeocraticy but there is nothing better when it all works out and you make a connection with that one student succeeds or a disagreement with a collegue ends up with something new and wonderful. In addition as the Chief of English Services at Project Genius, I assist my company's teachers, office staff, and owner with a variety of curriculum and interpersonal issues. In that capacity I have overseen short term courses, substitute with little notice, guest teach at schools, and interview and help place teachers at new client-schools.
  • 教育とカリキュラム設計 — 中高生や成人向けに、英語・ライティング・ディベート・留学準備などのプログラムを設計・指導しています。
  • Translation & Editing — I work with Japanese and English texts, from professional reports to creative writing.
  • 翻訳と編集 — 日本語・英語の専門文書から文章作品まで幅広く対応しています。
